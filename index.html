Our Story

When we went to look at cabinets for our new home, it was one bad experience after another. Sometimes when we visited showrooms, we were given very few choices, poor service, or found that the cabinets we loved cost far beyond what we budgeted.

One year after we purchased our cabinets, the paint started peeling off of them. However, we were told that the cabinets were only guaranteed for one year, and that there was nothing they could do without charging us extra.

We started Imperial Cabinets and Home Design because we know what it feels like to try to find the right pieces for a beautiful home. Our Lifetime Warranty policy was inspired by our experiences after we brought our cabinets home. We understand what makes really makes a home feel like a home.

Services

We offer a wide range of US-made and imported cabinets, from custom-designed to classic styles, to suit every room in your home. We specialize in cabinetry and closets for the kitchen, bath, and home office. We also office a custom design service, so that you can design your own furnishings to perfectly match your home.

A Lifetime Warranty is available for select products, as well as field measuring and custom services for a nominal fee. In most cases, we wave design fees once you place an order.
